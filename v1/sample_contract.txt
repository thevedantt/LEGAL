EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Agreement is made between Company A and Employee B.

1. Confidentiality
The employee shall not disclose any confidential information of the company during or after employment.

2. Liability
The company shall not be liable for any damages arising from the employee’s actions.

3. Termination
Either party may terminate this agreement by giving 30 days written notice.

4. Indemnity
The employee agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the company against any claims or losses.
